FAQ


What results can we expect with SEO services?

When you partner with Savit Interactive as your SEO agency, you can rest assured of getting access to thorough research, SEO site audits, technical SEO checks, and prompt addressing of issues like indexing, page speed, content, navigation, images and more. Your website will gain domain credibility from the search engine perspective while providing users with a smooth experience. These SEO services along with 100% transparency from our team will collectively drive relevant traffic and quality leads to your website.

How do you implement various strategies for our brand?

Savit Interactive has a team of highly experienced professionals who do not hesitate to think out of the box and create impactful strategies. To implement those strategies, we follow a step-by-step process of creating a list of keywords that you wish to target, perform an in-depth analysis of your website, perform a competitor analysis, optimise your website with white hat SEO practices, focus on content and design aspect of the site and improve its domain authority.

How long will it be until we can expect to see results?

SEO is a long-term process that grows over a period of time. The results that you observe on your website in 6 months will be lesser than the results you observe in 12 months. Depending on the results of your website audits, you may start to see impressive results in 6-8 months. These results indicate a considerable increase in traffic and progress associated with leads and conversions.

How can you incorporate SEO into our overall marketing strategy?

SEO consists of several elements and understanding what they are and how they work is an integral part of incorporating SEO in your overall marketing technique. SEO makes your website more visible to the audience that might be looking for a product or service related to yours. This ultimately means more traffic and better opportunities to convert your prospects into paying customers.

How do you stay up-to-date on industry changes and Google updates?

As the leading SEO agency in Mumbai, we need to stay up to date with the SEO trends and latest google updates. We ensure this by educating ourselves on the SEO trends, subscribing to SEO blogs, keeping track of the Google Webmaster Central blog and dig into social media updates to stay at par with the google updates and industry changes.
